Tennis

When it comes to sport, tennis holds a special place in my heart. I have been practising this sport since second grade and have played many tennis matches both in competitions and for fun. Some of my best memories were created on tennis courts, talking with friends, learning new techniques and enjoying the sheer thrill of the game. Tennis has taught me about perseverance, teamwork and cooperation. Through practising this sport, I have acquired many soft skills that are useful in my social and professional life in equal measure. I have improved upon my collaboration and communication skills in equal measure, working with team members to achieve a common goal: victory on the tennis field! Even though I have played both singles and doubles, I feel that playing a match with a second team member is more interactive! I feel a strong connection to this sport and hope to continue improving my tennis skills. I would like to join ESSEC's tennis team next year.
